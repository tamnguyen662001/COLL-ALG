COLL-ALG

COLL (Corners of the Last Layer) is an advanced extension to OLL. If the top cross is made, then OLL and the corners are solved at the same time. This forces one of four edge-only PLL algorithms, which makes PLL easier to recognize and faster to execute. Note: 2 sets (sune/antisune) are in the trash by default, as their COLL algorithms are too slow to be worth using.
